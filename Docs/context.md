## 2. Organisational Background

**BrainsForCompassion (BforC)** is a fully **women-led** social impact organisation that acts as a catalyst for change by partnering with NGOs, governments, corporations, and institutions striving to bring positive impact within communities.

Women from different parts of the world join hands with BforC to bring knowledge and wisdom under one roof — providing **global solutions to local problems**.

## 3. The Problem & Why This Platform Exists

India's social sector — comprising NGOs, social enterprises, and impact-driven organisations — faces a critical **talent gap**:

- Passionate individuals who want meaningful careers in social impact struggle to find the right opportunities.
- Organisations doing transformative work lack access to a dedicated talent pool that understands their mission.
- Existing job boards do not understand the social sector's unique needs.

**BforC exists to bridge this gap** — a dedicated ecosystem where compassion meets career, acting as connective tissue between purpose-driven professionals and the organisations that need their skills.

## 12. Design Philosophy

- **Women-Centric by Default:** Visual language conveying strength, warmth, and professionalism. Content and imagery reflecting the diversity of Indian women in professional settings.
- **Simplicity Without Compromise:** Clean, performant, and accessible — never cheap or bare.
- **Impact Over Vanity:** No bloated features. Every element serves the mission.
- **Zero-Cost Start, Scale with Purpose:** Free-tier infrastructure during startup; revenue funds organic upgrades.

## 13. UX & Non-Functional Requirements

### Performance
- Pages must load in **under 2 seconds on 3G networks**
- Images and assets must be optimized and cached aggressively
- API responses must feel instantaneous

### Accessibility
- **WCAG 2.1 AA** compliance minimum
- Full keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader compatibility
- Adequate color contrast ratios

### Mobile-First
- All design decisions start from the mobile experience
- Appropriate touch targets
- Forms optimized for mobile input
- Resume upload must work seamlessly on smartphones

### Trust & Transparency
- Clear privacy policies explaining resume data usage
- No hidden fees or dark patterns in subscription flows
- Employer verification to prevent fraudulent postings
- User data ownership — export or delete at any time

### Security
- HTTPS for all data transmission
- Resume malware scanning
- Secure authentication tokens
- Regular security audits

### Privacy
- GDPR-inspired data handling
- Minimal data collection
- No sale of user data
- Clear data retention policies

### Scalability
- Growth-ready database schema
- File storage optimization
- Edge-cached APIs
- Horizontal scaling readiness
