## 3. The Problem This Organisation Solves

India's social sector faces a critical **talent gap**:
- Passionate individuals wanting meaningful social impact careers can't find the right opportunities.
- Organisations doing transformative work lack a dedicated talent pool that understands their mission.
- Existing platforms don't understand the social sector's unique needs.

**BforC bridges this gap** — acting as connective tissue between purpose-driven professionals and the organisations that need their skills.

> 💡 This is context for copywriting and hero messaging. The career portal solves this technically; this marketing site communicates it emotionally.

## 10. Design Philosophy

- **Women-Centric by Default:** Visual language conveying strength, warmth, and professionalism — no stereotypes. Content and imagery reflecting the diversity of Indian women in professional settings.
- **Simplicity Without Compromise:** Clean, performant, and accessible — never cheap or bare.
- **Impact Over Vanity:** No bloated features. Every element communicates the mission.
- **Mobile-First:** Many Indian users access via smartphone. Design for mobile, then scale up.

### Tone of Voice
- Empowering, not pitying
- Professional, not corporate
- Warm, not casual
- Inclusive of all genders while centred on women's progress

---

## 11. UX & Performance Requirements

### Performance
- Pages load in **under 2 seconds on 3G**
- Aggressively optimize and cache images and assets

### Accessibility
- **WCAG 2.1 AA** minimum compliance
- Full keyboard navigation
- Screen reader compatible
- Correct color contrast ratios

### Mobile-First
- All design decisions start from mobile
- Appropriate touch targets
- Forms optimized for mobile input

### Trust Signals
- No dark patterns
- Clear privacy communication
- Employer verification badges visible on the site
