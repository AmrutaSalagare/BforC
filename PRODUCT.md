# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Donors, NGOs, local communities, and government institutions seeking strategy, capacity building, and collaborative interventions for social impact.

## Product Purpose
A landing page to introduce Brains for Compassion (BforC), establish credibility, share its origin story, list its services, showcase its partners, and provide a contact point for potential collaborators.

## Brand Personality
Compassionate, thoughtful, grounded, purposeful, quiet confidence, human-centric.

## Anti-references
- High-tech, noisy SaaS templates with glowing neon and glassmorphism.
- Sterile, corporate consultancy layouts with generic stock photos.
- Aggressive marketing pages with huge conversion buttons and loud pop-ups.

## Design Principles
- Quiet confidence: Lead with wisdom and restraint, not shouting.
- Human over metric: Focus on real relationships and outcomes, avoid treating stats as purely decorative.
- Warm and grounded: The design should feel tactile, real, and inviting, bridging global professionalism with local warmth.
- Clarity of purpose: Everything on the screen must serve the story or the next step. No arbitrary decorations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability across various screen qualities.
- Reduced motion alternatives available (calm, unforced animations).
- Clear, readable typography (WCAG AA compliant minimum).
